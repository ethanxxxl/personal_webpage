Overview

Hello and welcome to my website! Coming from the boondocks in Western Pennsylvania, I grew up with a passion for technology (ie, I'm a nerd). I started teaching myself programming when I was in middle school, an endeavor which continues to this day. During High School, I went to our local trade school and learned the basics of electrical engineering. I love the intersection of hardware and software, so much so that I decided to spend four years of my life studying actual engineering at Penn State. At Penn State, I am currently a Senior majoring in Electrical Engineering, with minors in Computer Engineering and Military Studies. Going to college also afforded me the opportunity to join the United States Air Force through AFROTC. I am slated to commission in May 2023 (Lord willing) as a 2nd Lt, hopefully as an Electrical Developmental Engineer (62EX)
